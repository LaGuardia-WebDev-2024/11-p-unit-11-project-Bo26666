My Top Movies of This Year

  1. How I Met Your Mother

    This is my all time favorite show. Its 9 seasons with about 24 episodes per season which I love because it quickly became my everything show. I watched it in the background while I was cooking, doing schoolwork, painting, etc, but its not some boring background show I would also watch it without distractions and be fully invested. Eventhough its such a long show it doesnt feel dragged out at all. evey episode is needed. All the characters are so loveable and you get to know all of them very well. In addition to all this, watching this show can give you great relationship as well as just regular life advice and eventhough its a comedy show you are able to feel deeper emotions while watching.

  2. A Bronx Tale

    This movie is so meaningful to me, not only because of the beautiful love story, but because it feels so close to me. Let me explain what I mean: this movie was filmed in the Bronx which is where I am from. watching this movie I see everything that has changed, stayed the same, and also the buses I take and very meaning spots to me. Its really cool to see the history of my own neighborhood and watch a meaninful love story in a setting I know and love so well. Secondly, The story involves two teenages of different racial backgrounds falling inlove, despite warnings from family and friends and critisism/looks from others. This is also imapactful to me because I am a mixed race child and hear the stories
