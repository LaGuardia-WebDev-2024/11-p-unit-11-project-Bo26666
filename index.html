My Top Movies of This Year

  1. Boy

    The story follows an 11-year-old Māori boy named Boy, who idolizes his absent father, imagining him as a heroic, Michael Jackson–like figure. Boy lives with his grandmother, younger brother Rocky (who believes he has magical powers), and cousins.

  2. Forrest Gump

    Forrest Gump is a sweeping drama that follows the life of a kind-hearted man with a low IQ as he unknowingly influences major events in American history.

  3. The Iron Claw

    The story centers on Kevin Von Erich (played by Zac Efron), one of several brothers trained by their strict, controlling father, Fritz Von Erich. The brothers—Kevin, David, Kerry, and Mike—rise to fame in professional wrestling during the 1970s and 1980s, chasing championships and their father’s approval.
